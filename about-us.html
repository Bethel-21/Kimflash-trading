Who We are

We Are KIMFLASH Trading

Kimflash has the experience and expertise to design and install high performance solar energy systems that can dramatically lower, or even eliminate, energy costs for your home or business. Our custom fit solar solutions guarantee a cleaner, greener future and ensure a sound investment that could save you big in the long term.

Kimflash Trading Plc is formed with the intention of reducing the gap in the market in the renewable energy sector and our engineers and marketers who have vast experiences, extensive knowledge of technology and the customer's requirement and old hands in the industry will make the gap narrowed. The resultant of the skill, competency and the domain knowledge will enable us to offer the best second to none solution in the sector in Ethiopia.

We ensure customer satisfaction through best business practices, credible technical skills, support system and sustainable elective partnerships for prompt delivery of solutions.
